Dealership Service

Need to take your car into the dealership? Don’t do it yourself, we can handle the whole process for you. We’ll make all the arrangements, take your car in and bring it back to you when the service is complete.

$60 Service Fee

Time: Varies

Service Terms

  • Only offered for services covered under vehicle warranty
  • Appointment times vary based on dealership availability; we’ll try to schedule an appointment as close to your requested time as possible and confirm the final arrangements with you
  • Timing varies based on services requested; we’ll communicate with the dealership and provide you with an ETA upon vehicle drop-off
  • We’ll provide you with updates if the ETA changes while your car is at the dealership
  • If the dealership recommends additional services during the process, we’ll contact you for approval prior to starting any additional work

What's Included

  • We book the service appointment for you
  • We pick up your vehicle, take it to the dealership and drop it back off after
  • We have the dealership perform the services/maintenance you request
  • We collect the paperwork for you and ensure all the requested work has been completed
